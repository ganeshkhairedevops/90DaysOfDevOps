## Quick Concepts

### OSI vs TCP/IP
- OSI is a conceptual model (Layer 1–7)
- TCP/IP is the practical implementation

Mapping:
- OSI L1–L2 → Link
- OSI L3 → Internet
- OSI L4 → Transport
- OSI L5–L7 → Application
